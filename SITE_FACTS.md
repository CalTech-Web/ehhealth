### 7. Abortion Support

## FAQ Content

**Q: Is doula care really covered by my Medi-Cal insurance?**
Yes. EH Nursing and Wellness Services is a Medi-Cal approved doula provider in Los Angeles. If you have a Medi-Cal Managed Care plan such as LA Care, Kaiser, HealthNet, or CalOptima, you may qualify for covered doula services at no out-of-pocket cost. Contact us with your plan details and we will help you verify your coverage.

**Q: What is the difference between a doula and a medical provider?**
A doula provides non-medical emotional, physical, and educational support throughout pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. Nurse Ellen brings deep clinical experience as a Pediatric and Neonatal ICU nurse and Nurse Practitioner, but her doula services are complementary to, not a replacement for, your medical team. She works alongside your OB, midwife, or care team.

**Q: Can I reach a real person if I have questions?**
Yes. During normal business hours you can always call (833) 912-3999 and speak directly with someone on our team. We prioritize real, human connection and will never leave you waiting for an automated response when you need support.

**Q: Do you support VBAC (vaginal birth after cesarean)?**
Yes. VBAC support is one of our specialties. Nurse Ellen provides evidence-informed VBAC preparation, coping strategies, and collaborative communication with your care team so you can make informed decisions and feel confident going into your birth.

**Q: Do you provide support for pregnancy loss, miscarriage, or abortion?**
Yes. We offer sensitive, non-judgmental support for families experiencing miscarriage, stillbirth, or abortion. We respect your privacy, your pace, and your choices, and are here to provide emotional support and practical resources during a difficult time.

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
We serve families throughout Van Nuys and greater Los Angeles County. As a mobile provider, Nurse Ellen comes to you. There is no office visit required.

**Q: How do I get started?**
Call (833) 912-3999 or fill out the contact form at ehhealth.org/contact-us. Share your due date, your Medi-Cal plan, and the type of support you are looking for. We will reach out to schedule your initial assessment visit.

## Key Messaging / Brand Voice

- Accessibility first: "Insurance covered doula care" is the primary hook. Medi-Cal coverage removes the financial barrier that keeps many families from professional doula support.
- Nurse-led credibility: Ellen's background as a Pediatric and NICU nurse and Nurse Practitioner gives clinical weight to her doula work, even though the services themselves are non-medical.
- Real human connection: Clients can always reach a real person by phone during business hours. This is a core differentiator against larger, more impersonal services.
- Non-judgmental and inclusive: Every family is welcomed without judgment, including LGBTQ+ families, teen parents, families navigating loss, and those in vulnerable circumstances.
- Continuity of care: One consistent provider from prenatal through postpartum, not a rotating team or anonymous service.
- Evidence-backed outcomes: Doula support is linked to 4x lower rates of low birth weight, 2x lower complication rates, lower C-section rates, and higher breastfeeding rates.
- Compassion-forward voice: Copy uses words like "warmth," "steady presence," "non-judgmental," "deeply cared for." Avoid clinical coldness. Speak to emotions first, then credentials.
